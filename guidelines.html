Uploading Guidelines

  • NSFW/NSFL (Not Safe For Work/Life) content (porn of any kind (legal or not), gore, etc) is absolutely not allowed, and if your account is found to have uploaded this kind of video, you can face: video deletion and strike, account deletion, temporary IP ban, or permanent IP ban.

  • Additionally, corrupted videos, defined as videos that cannot be played, will be deleted.

  • You cannot upload more than 4 videos per day, if you upload 5 or more videos in one day you will receive a community guidelines strike.

  • Don't upload the same video multiple times, it will result in a community guidelines strike.

Commenting Guidelines

  • Leaving multiple comments with no purpose or spamming will get your comment(s) deleted.

  • Posting links to your videos or profiles (links that lead off-platform included) is not allowed and carries the same punishment as above.

Strike System

  • Generally, an infraction (instance of breaking one of these rules) will give you 1 strike.

  • Unless it's something more severe (illegal content for example), you'll only get one strike for every infraction.

  • If you have 2 strikes, you lose your ability to upload/comment (depending on which rules you broke).

  • When your account reaches 3 strikes, it will be deactivated (meaning you will no longer be able to login), and all your videos will be hidden (or deleted, if they violated these guidelines).

  • Generally you can appeal these decisions. But depending on what you did and the severity of it, appeals may not be accepted.
